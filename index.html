1. Introduction

DashhFIT ("we", "our", "the App") is a personal fitness and nutrition tracking application available on iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

By using DashhFIT, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

Account & Identity
  • Telegram user ID (used as your account identifier)
  • First name or display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional, selected from your device gallery)
Physical & Health Data (entered voluntarily, never required)
  • Body weight (kg), height (cm), age, gender
  • Body fat percentage
  • Body measurements: chest, bicep, forearm, waist, thigh, calf, glutes, neck (cm)
  • Fitness goal (lose fat / build muscle / health / sport performance)
  • Fitness level (beginner / intermediate / advanced)
  • Desired training frequency (sessions per week)
Workout Data
  • Workout sessions: date, status, exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration, notes
  • Workout templates: name, scheduled days, exercise lists
  • Rest timer usage and actual rest durations
Nutrition Data
  • Food entries: name, description, calories, protein, carbs, fat, meal type, time logged
  • Daily water intake logs
  • Daily calorie and macro goals
AI Chat Messages
  • Messages you send to the AI coach
  • AI responses (stored locally on your device only)

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Authentication token — a signed JWT stored in your device's encrypted secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • Expo push token — generated locally for notifications, never shared with advertising networks
  • Subscription status — plan type and expiry date, cached locally for offline use

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

Never collected
  • GPS or precise location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID)
  • Browsing history or cross-app activity
  • Biometric raw data (Face ID / Touch ID — OS-level only, no raw data accessed)
  • Financial details (card numbers, bank accounts — payments handled entirely by YooKassa)
  • Any data from children under 13 years of age

4. Data Storage

4.1 On Your Device (Local)

The following data is stored exclusively on your device in an encrypted SQLite database and never leaves it:

  • All workout sessions and templates
  • All nutrition entries and water logs
  • All AI chat sessions and messages
  • Your profile data (basics, measurements, history)
  • App settings and preferences
  • JWT authentication token (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • Subscription status cache (encrypted, 7-day TTL)

4.2 On Our Servers (Supabase, EU — Frankfurt)

  • Subscription records: Telegram ID, plan type, activation date, expiry date
  • AI usage counters: Telegram ID, daily request count (free-tier enforcement)
  • Generated workout plans: deleted after you import them (or after 30 days)
  • Bot survey answers: deleted immediately after plan generation
  • Profile sync: basic fields synced from bot survey answers only

8. AI Features — Special Disclosure

8.1 Food Photo Analysis

  1. Image compressed on your device (70% quality)
  2. Sent over HTTPS to our API server
  3. Forwarded to IO Intelligence's vision model
  4. Deleted immediately — not stored anywhere after analysis
  5. API returns only: food name, calories, protein, carbs, fat

8.2 AI Workout Plan Generation

Your anonymised fitness profile (no name, no Telegram username) is sent to the AI model. Survey answers are deleted immediately after the plan is generated. The plan is stored temporarily until you import it.

8.3 AI Coach Chat

Your messages are sent with an aggregated summary of your recent workouts and nutrition (numbers only, no photos, no personally identifying information). Chat history is stored only on your device — we do not retain a server copy.

9. Payments & Subscriptions

All payments are processed by YooKassa. The flow: you tap "Get Premium" → our server creates a YooKassa payment → you pay on YooKassa's secure page → YooKassa confirms via webhook → your subscription activates. DashhFIT never sees your card details.

11. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data directly in the app (Profile section)
  • Rectification: Edit any data at any time within the app
  • Deletion: Profile → Delete Account — deletes local data and queues server-side deletion within 30 days
  • Portability: Request a data export by contacting us
  • Opt-out of notifications: Profile → Settings → Notifications — configurable per type
  • Opt-out of AI features: All AI features are opt-in — simply don't use them
  • GDPR (EU/EEA): Legal basis is Contract and Consent. Right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority
  • CCPA (California): We do not sell personal information. Contact us to exercise your rights

12. Data Security

  • In transit: HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ on all connections
  • At rest (device): iOS Keychain / Android Keystore for tokens; SQLite in app's private sandbox
  • At rest (server): AES-256 encryption via Supabase; row-level security per user
  • Authentication: JWT HS256, token length capped at 2048 bytes, rate limiting on all endpoints
  • Webhooks: Telegram webhook verified by secret token; YooKassa webhook verified by IP whitelist

13. Automated Decision-Making

AI features (food analysis, workout plans, chat) are automated but produce no legal or similarly significant effects. All results are suggestions — you retain full control and can override or ignore them.
